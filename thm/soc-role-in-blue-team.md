# THM - SOC Role in Blue Team
**Platform:** TryHackMe

## What It Covered
Overview of SOC structure, analyst levels, and how 
to progress in a security operations career.

## What I Learned
- SOC has tiered roles: L1 triage, L2 investigation, 
  L3 threat hunting
- Career progression requires both technical depth 
  and soft skills
- Blue team work is continuous — threats never stop
