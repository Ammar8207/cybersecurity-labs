# THM - Defensive Security Intro
**Platform:** TryHackMe

## What It Covered
Introduction to the blue team side of cybersecurity — 
threat intelligence, SOC operations, DFIR, and SIEM basics.

## What I Learned
- Defensive security is about preventing and responding 
  to attacks, not just detecting them
- SOC teams monitor networks 24/7 for suspicious activity
- SIEM tools aggregate logs to help analysts spot threats
